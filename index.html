About the Company

Founded in 1967 as a one-man operation, our company has grown into a third-generation family business specializing in pastry and bakery paper packaging. What began with a single craftsman's dedication has evolved into a trusted name in the industry—combining tradition with innovation. For over five decades, we've stayed at the forefront of the field by actively participating in expos and industry events, always staying in tune with market trends and customer needs. Our commitment to quality, service, and sustainability continues to shape our journey till this day.
